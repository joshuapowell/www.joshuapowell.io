Joshua Powell –
is a designer, developer, farmer, cyclist, surfer, camper, husband, father, and lives in Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania.

Joshua is currently serving as Partner and Chief Technology Officer at Viable Industries.

In 2008, Joshua founded Developed Simple a web design and data management shop in Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania which he ran until he founded Viable Industries in 2013.

He’s a part-time vegetable farmer with the support of his wife. He founded Farm Small in 2016 to provide locally grown produce to the communities he works and lives in.

Joshua lives in Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania with his wife and three children.
