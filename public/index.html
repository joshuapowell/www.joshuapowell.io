Joshua Powell –

is a designer, developer, farmer, cyclist, surfer, camper, husband, father, and lives in Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania.

Joshua is currently serving as Partner and Chief Technology Officer at Viable Industries, where he develops open-source software.

In 2008, he founded Developed Simple a data management consultancy in Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania which he ran until he founded Viable Industries in 2013.

He’s a part-time vegetable farmer and founded Farm Small in 2016 to provide locally grown produce to the communities he works and lives in.

He's the creator and core contributor to Commons Cloud, a platform that provides crowd-sourced data hosting and visualization for environmental organizations. He creates other open source tools which he distributes through Github and contributes to projects like OpenStreetMap.

He lives in Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania with his wife and three children.
